BUILDING

53 roster spots. One salary cap. Infinite decisions. The front offices that master this puzzle, through the draft, free agency, and extensions, build the dynasties of tomorrow.

THREE PATHS TO ROSTER BUILDING

📋
The Draft

The foundation of every sustainable roster. Elite front offices identify talent early, trade up when necessary, and consistently develop late-round picks into starters. The draft is how you build while staying under the cap.

🤝
Free Agency

A tool for filling specific holes, not rebuilding. Championship teams use free agency surgically, targeting veterans who fit their scheme and culture. Overspending in free agency is the fastest road to cap trouble.

✍️
Extensions

Keeping your own players before they hit the open market is the most underrated skill in team building. Pre-market extensions keep cap hits manageable and lock in the culture builders of your locker room.

MAXIMIZING EVERY DRAFT PICK

Rounds 1 and 2
Cornerstone Players

Swing for elite starters. These picks become the core of your roster for the next decade. Never reach: trade back if the value isn't there.

Round 3
Immediate Contributors

The third round produces more starting-caliber players than any other. Teams that hit on Day 2 picks consistently build playoff rosters.

Rounds 4 and 5
Scheme Fits

Target players who fit your specific system. A scheme-perfect 4th round pick outperforms a talented player who doesn't fit your culture.

Rounds 6 and 7
High-Upside Gambles

Go for ceiling. Raw athleticism and character who need development. Tom Brady was a 6th round pick. Late rounds reward vision.
