# Building A Championship NFL Team

A multi-page website breaking down the five pillars behind every NFL dynasty — quarterback play, defense, coaching, roster construction, and culture.
---

## Pages
- **Home** — Introduction and overview
- **About** — Research and methodology
- **QB Role** — Why the quarterback position wins championships
- **Defense** — Building a dominant defensive unit
- **Coaching** — Leadership principles of the greatest coaches
- **Roster** — Draft strategy, free agency, and cap management

---

## Built With
- HTML5 & CSS3
- Google Fonts (Bebas Neue, Barlow)
